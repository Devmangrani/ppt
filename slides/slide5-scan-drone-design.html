Component Details

1

Airframe & Propulsion

750 mm carbon-fiber frame with T-Motor 5006 (320 KV) motors, 17-inch propellers, and an in-house developed 40 A ESC, optimized for low-RPM operation, high efficiency, and stable hover during long scouting missions.

2

Battery Chemistry & Configuration

Powered by 18650 Li-ion cells in a 6S3P configuration (22.2 V nominal), selected for high energy density, stable discharge behavior, and improved endurance compared to conventional Li-Po batteries.

3

Power & Control Electronics

An in-house built power module provides regulated power rails, voltage and current sensing, and electrical noise isolation, ensuring reliable operation of the flight controller and onboard peripherals.

4

Navigation & Positioning

Uses an M10 GNSS module with multi-constellation support, enabling faster position lock, accurate waypoint navigation, and reliable geo-tagging during autonomous scanning missions.

5

Endurance & Mission Focus

With an average hover power of ~450–500 W and usable energy of ~200–220 Wh, the drone achieves an estimated 35-40 minutes of flight time, optimized for crop scouting and data acquisition.
