Current Agricultural Challenges

Labor Shortage

Severe agricultural workforce reduction due to rapid urban migration, making manual monitoring of large fields impractical and expensive.

Soil Degradation

Blanket spraying of pesticides causes long-term soil toxicity, reduces biodiversity, and increases chemical residue in food products.

Technology Gap

Lack of affordable precision agriculture tools prevents small-to-medium farmers from optimizing yields and reducing input costs.
