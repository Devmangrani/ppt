Component Details

1

Airframe & Propulsion

950 mm carbon-fiber frame equipped with T-Motor 6007 (320 KV) motors, 21-inch carbon-fiber propellers, and an in-house developed 40 A ESC, optimized for high lift efficiency, low-RPM operation, and stable hover under payload.

2

Battery Chemistry & Configuration

Powered by 21700 Li-ion cells (Molicel P42A) in a 6S10P configuration (22.2 V nominal), selected for high current capability, reduced voltage sag, and extended endurance during payload-carrying missions.

3

Power & Control Electronics

An in-house built power module provides regulated power rails, real-time voltage and current sensing, and electrical noise suppression to ensure reliable operation of the flight controller, pumps, and communication systems.

4

Navigation & Positioning

Integrated with an M10 GNSS module supporting multi-constellation navigation, enabling accurate waypoint tracking, stable position hold, and reliable autonomous operation over large agricultural fields.

5

Endurance & Mission Focus

With an average hover power of ~800–850 W and usable energy of ~900 Wh, the drone achieves an estimated 55–60 minutes of flight time, optimized for precision spraying and long-duration action missions.
